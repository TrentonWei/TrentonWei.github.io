    • Personal Profile

    Personal Profile： I have long been engaged in fundamental research related to geovisualization and GeoAI. In recent years, I have published over 50 academic papers in high-level domestic and international journals and academic conferences, including PR, ISPRS P&RS, IJGIS, JEM, CaGIS, IJDE, ATMOS RES, TGIS, JoV, GeoCarto, 《计算机辅助设计与图形学学报》、《测绘学报》和《武汉大学学报 （信息科学版）》. I have also applied for 6 invention patents. I serve as an editorial board member for the international journal Scientific Reports (starting from 2025) and have acted as a reviewer for over two dozen mainstream academic journals, such as ISPRS P&RS, AUTCON, PR, INFFUS, IJGIS, NEUCOM, IEEE J-STARS, CaGIS, TGIS, 《测绘学报》和《武汉大学学报（信息科学版）》. I have been honored with the titles of "Excellent Reviewer of 2024" and "Candidate for Best Paper of 2024" by 《测绘学报》.

    I'm always looking for highly self-motivated PhD/master students, visiting students, and undergraduate interns (RAs) to work with me. Please directly send your CV to me (trentonwei [AT] whu.edu.cn) if you find my research interesting.
